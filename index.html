About Mduduzi Ncube

Mduduzi Ncube is a South African Afro Pop singer and songwriter, born and raised in Hammarsdale,Kwazulu-Natal. He got his first big break when DJ Tira heard his talent and recorded his first single titled ‘MALOKAZI’ featuring Berita. Current mduduzi is under Nkabi records.
